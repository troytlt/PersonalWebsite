About Me

Hello World!

My name is Tran Le Thanh Tri. I was born and raised in Ho Chi Minh city, Vietnam. I graduated from the Open University of Malaysia with a bachelor degree in Finance. I spent years working as an auditor for a “big-four” auditing firm in the world, risk operation officer in banking industry and financial analyst for a thousand-billion-dong worth research company in Vietnam. Most importantly, I embarked on my part-time English teaching career at a very young age. I worked my way through school being a math tutor, IELTS teacher and freelancer writer in an academic program.

I believe in the perpetuity of education that one’s learning path never stops after college. And that is something that drives me to pick up new skills and widen my horizons at the best of my effort. When the Fintech industry (which is short for Finance and technology) thrives, I acquired programming languages and coding skills. I can write interactive websites, and programs.
